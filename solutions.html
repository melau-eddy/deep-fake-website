Media Literacy & Public Awareness

Education remains our most powerful defense against deepfake deception. By training users to critically evaluate media, we can reduce susceptibility to synthetic disinformation.

  • Recognize subtle facial distortions and unnatural blinking
  • Check for inconsistent lighting and shadows
  • Verify audio sync with mouth movements
  • Look for digital artifacts around edges
  • Cross-reference with trusted sources
